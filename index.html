Why Honest Reviews Matter

Social media is flooded with sponsored content forcing YouTubers to push the most expensive apps, software, tools, e-bikes, and computers—products that exceed what you actually need. Truth-telling has gone missing. Being real, honest, and direct is rare.

Fabien's Critique was born from frustration with this BS. If there's something that can prevent you from overspending, welcome to the club. Most people don't need a Ferrari to go to the grocery store—they need a reliable car to get to work.

I've spent years testing products, experiencing intense rides, and visiting unique venues. Here, you get my unfiltered perspective—the adrenaline rushes, the disappointments, and everything in between.

No sponsorships. No affiliate pressure. Just authentic experiences that save you money and help you make smarter decisions.
